Enquiry FAQs

What information do I need for a wholesale enquiry?

For wholesale enquiries, please provide your business registration details, estimated monthly order volume, and information about your retail space or online store. This helps us create the best proposal for your business.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantity for wholesale is R2,500. For bulk orders and corporate gifts, we can accommodate smaller quantities starting from R1,000. Custom orders may have different requirements.

Do you offer custom product development?

Yes! We specialize in custom product development for brands and corporate clients. Minimum quantities apply, and the process typically takes 4-6 weeks from concept to delivery.

What support do you provide to retail partners?

We provide comprehensive support including marketing materials, product training, display units, and co-marketing opportunities. Our team works closely with partners to ensure success.
