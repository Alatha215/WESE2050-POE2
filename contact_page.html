Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within South Africa takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for delivery within 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by location but typically take 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused products in their original packaging. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team to initiate a return. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes! All Seemah Gloss products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan. We never test on animals, and all our ingredients are plant-based. We're proud to be certified by both Leaping Bunny and Vegan Action.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can view the exact shipping cost for your location during checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can use this to track your package's journey to your doorstep. If you have any issues with tracking, please contact our customer service team.
