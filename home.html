Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Sarah Johnson, Seemah Gloss began as a passion project in a small Johannesburg apartment. What started as homemade lip balms for friends and family quickly grew into a movement for ethical beauty.

Today, we're proud to be one of South Africa's leading cruelty-free beauty brands, serving customers across the globe who believe that beauty shouldn't come at the expense of our values.
