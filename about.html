Our Story

Where shine meets power - Discover the journey behind South Africa's premier cruelty-free beauty brand

From Passion to Purpose

Founded in 2020 by Sarah Johnson, Seemah Gloss began as a passion project in a small Johannesburg apartment. Frustrated by the lack of high-quality, cruelty-free lip products in the South African market, Sarah started experimenting with natural ingredients and sustainable formulations.

What started as homemade lip balms for friends and family quickly grew into a movement. Today, we're proud to be one of South Africa's leading cruelty-free beauty brands, serving customers across the globe who believe that beauty shouldn't come at the expense of our values.

Every product we create is infused with our commitment to quality, sustainability, and empowerment. We believe that when you feel good about what you put on your lips, you shine with confidence from within.

Sarah Johnson

Founder & CEO

Our Mission

"To create premium, cruelty-free beauty products that empower confidence, celebrate individuality, and make a positive impact on our world."

We're committed to proving that luxury beauty can be ethical, sustainable, and accessible to everyone. Through innovative formulations, responsible sourcing, and community engagement, we're redefining what it means to be beautiful.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through eco-friendly packaging, responsible sourcing, and carbon-neutral shipping.

Cruelty-Free

All our products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan. We never test on animals and are certified by Leaping Bunny and Vegan Action.

Inclusivity

Beauty comes in all shades. Our products are designed to celebrate diversity and make everyone feel seen and beautiful.

Quality

We use only the finest ingredients and rigorous testing to ensure every product meets our high standards of excellence.
