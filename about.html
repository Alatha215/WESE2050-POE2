Who We Are

Seemah Gloss was born out of our desire to create a lip gloss brand that truly reflects the beauty and diversity of modern beauty culture. We wanted to create formulas that are not only deliciously smooth and moisturizing but also cruelty-free, vegan-friendly and made with love.

From our signature shade "Champagne Glow", to our limited-edition collaborations with beauty influencers, every aspect of Seemah Gloss is created to celebrate the beauty of self-expression. Whether you're a lip gloss fanatic or just starting to explore the endless world of makeup, we would love to invite you to join our Seemah Gloss family.

Our Mission

Empowering people to express themselves through the power of lip gloss because we at Seemah Gloss believe that a swipe of our product can boost confidence, ignite creativity and bring people together.
